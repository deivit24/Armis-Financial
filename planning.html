Advance Planning

At Armis Financial, we know that financial planning may be broad or focused based on your needs. If you have ever asked your self these questions, let's get acquainted.

  1. Which investments should I choose for my 401(k)?
  2. Which of my employee benefits do I need to take advantage of?
  3. Can I afford to send my kids to a great college?
  4. I'm an independent contractor and need to set up benefits and a retirement plan. What should I do?
  5. How do I counter-punch all these enormous taxes I pay?
  6. Will my family be ok if I “kick the bucket” early in life or become disabled?
  7. Will I be able to retire?
  8. How much income will you need in retirement?
  9. Are you spending too much in fees?
  10. Is your asset allocation appropriate given your financial goals?
  11. Does your income support your spending/saving goals?
  12. How should you prioritize debt payments?

Armis Financial meets with you two to three times online via web services, in person, or over the telephone to dig into these topics and how they relate to you. We will discuss your current situation, our analysis findings, and our proposed recommendations in developing your financial plan. At the end of the process, we will deliver a written summary of the plan and recommendations for you to follow and work with you to implement the proposed recommendation. The time to complete a plan depends upon the complexity of your situation and how quickly you can provide data to us, however, most plans can be completed within one month.
